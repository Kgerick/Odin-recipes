French Fries

About the Dish

This dish is a great side for lunch, dinner, and even certain breakfasts. It has a buttery and herby garnish that will absolutely tickle your taste buds! Its a quick make too! So lets jump into it!

What You Will Need

  • Russet Potato
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425.
  2. Start by Shaving the potatos and tossing the shavings away.
  3. Next you're going to want to cut the potatos into long thin rectangles.
  4. Once you have done that toss them into a large bowl and and half a cup of all of the spices.
  5. Shake the bowl well and make sure all of the fries are coated.
  6. Lay out on baking sheet and place in oven.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes.
