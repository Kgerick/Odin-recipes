Salsa

About the dish

This is a great dish for everything going on in your life! Whether its a party or a casual snack it could even a side to your main dish. This salsa is a nice non-chunky delicious cilantro based version of your favorite snack!

What You Will Need

  • 8oz can of rotel tomatoes and chiles
  • Diced tomatoes
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • Honey
  • Salt
  • Chips

Steps

  1. First dump your can of rotel and can of diced tomatoes into your blender.
  2. Next chop up your cilantro bunch and add it to your blender.
  3. Add a teaspoon of honey into the blender
  4. Next add a dash of salt and blend. Add more salt until content with the salt levels.
